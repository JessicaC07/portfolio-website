About, Education
& Testimonials

Hi there!

I am Jessica. I have a French and Chinese background that led me to be very curious and passionate about travels and languages. I am bilingual French - English, fluent in Spanish and can understand Chinese Mandarin.

I was always interested in our digital environment, so I completed a Master's degree in marketing and started combining both passions - I worked in Montreal, Barcelona, New York City & Vancouver in digital marketing, as well as freelancing since 2018.

I quickly realized I love working on the technical aspect of my roles - technical SEO, email automation, but especially editing and optimizing websites. This led me to enroll in the Front-End Web Development Certificate at BCIT, Vancouver which I completed in June 2022.

I deepened my HTML/ CSS knowledge and learning so much, especially more advanced web technologies such as React and the Javascript ecosystem. The program also taught us design/ UX, as well as backend development with PHP/ WordPress, and ecommerce platforms such as Shopify and WooCommerce.

Oh, I have two other hobbies - food & red wine!
